Section4.11Practical Percent Problems

There are three basic types of percent problems:

1. Determine a certain percent of a given number. For example, find \(25\%\) of \(640\). 2. Determine a percent given two numbers. For example, \(15\) is what percent of \(50\)? 3. Determine a number that is a given percent of another number. For example, \(10\%\) of what number is \(12\)?
