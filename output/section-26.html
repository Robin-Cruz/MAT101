Section4.12Percent Increase or Decrease

A person's salary can increase by a percentage. A town's population can decrease by a percentage. A clothing firm can discount its apparel. Consider the following examples.

Example4.12.1 Percent Increase

A salesperson is granted a 5% salary increase. If the salesperson's current salary is \(\$4,000\) per month, what will be her new salary?

Example4.12.2 Percent Decrease

Due to a mill closure, the population of Silvertown decreases by \(8.5\%\). If the original population was \(10,200\) hardy souls, what is the new population?
