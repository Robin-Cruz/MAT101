As a decimal, \(23\) parts out of \(100\) is \(23\%=\dfrac{23}{100}=0.23\). We see that \(100\%=\dfrac{100}{100}=1\) is all \(100\) parts out of \(100\). This means \(0.23\) is a number less than \(1\) but greater than \(0\) since \(0=\dfrac{0}{100}\) zero parts out of \(100\).
